About AmlaDrop

AmlaDrop is Amla's secure internal temporary email platform designed for email verification, OTP validation, password reset testing, and end-to-end automation scenarios.

Built specifically for development, QA, and automation teams, AmlaDrop eliminates the need for third-party disposable email services such as YOPmail or Mailinator by providing a secure, organization-controlled email testing solution.

All temporary messages are retained for 24 hours, allowing teams to validate workflows, troubleshoot automation failures, and verify notifications before automatic cleanup.

Temporary email inboxes for testing and verification
OTP, MFA, and password reset validation
Designed for QA and automation frameworks
Secure internal alternative to YOPmail and Mailinator
